Syst'aime
Printmaking
2022

戀愛體誌  Syst'aime


is a satirical tabloid that chronicles materialized relationships in modern society through the lens of a fictional journalist. By presenting four headline stories and twelve physical evidences, it reveals the contradictions between secular rules and romantic feelings shaped by hidden values and societal norms.


20 pages, 22x17, screen printing

Winner, Red Dot Design Award 2022


Category / print, behavioral design, satirical design

Treasure Zone
Brand Identity
2021

尋聚地  Treasure Zone


is the theme of the Taipei Toys Festival, transforming the exhibition into an undefined conceptual space, where the exhibits become hidden treasures. Playful symbols are used to represent the acts of searching, gathering, and the divergence of signals, suggesting that the possibilities are as limitless as the creativity and imagination of the visitors.

03

Taipei Girls' High School
Brand Identity
2021

Taipei Girls' High School


is the most prestigious and historically significant girls' high school in Taiwan, established in 1904. By redesigning its visual system, the aim is to alleviate the public's perception of the school's conservative image and make its brand more vibrant and lively. The supporting graphics are inspired by the school's architectural features from the Japanese colonial period, while maintaining the iconic uniform color as the primary color scheme.



Category / branding, visual identity design

Into the Unknown
Poster
2023

美麗新世界  Into the Unknown


is a sentimental short film that revolves around Gao, a female worker in a textile factory, who dreams of becoming a pastry chef. Outside of her work, Gao strives to save her wages to afford ingredients for her baking practice. As she navigates this journey, her heart is torn between the looming presence of a factory supervisor and the enduring companionship of a childhood friend, each person offering a different form of love that she hopes will fill the emptiness within her.



TNUA Department of Filmmaking Graduation Project

Director/ Shih Jia, Huang

07

Thoughts in the Sky
Creative Coding
2024

Thoughts in the Sky


is an interactive website that creates an environment for uploading your thoughts into the air. When you casually stroll down the road alone, where do your thoughts go? The words that dance through your mind seem to dissolve into the air, light as bubbles, floating up to join the clouds. They drift in silence, playing with the wind, forming fleeting shapes against the sky, as if the world itself becomes a canvas for your inner dialogue.



Category / creative coding, interaction design

08

The Distance of Missing
Creative Coding
2024

The Distance of Missing


is a conceptual data display website that builds upon a real-world database of airlines and airport locations, weaving together routes that span the globe and cross hemispheres. It captures the journey of how people, scattered across vast distances, find their way back to the places they once called home, reconnecting with their lost sense of belonging. This project offers a poetic narrative of movement and the connections between humans and places.



Category / creative coding, data visualization, interaction design

Creative Coding
2024

Archaeology of Memory

(click any item to visit the project site!)


is a digital project that begins with an exploration of the word "archive." One lesser-known but valid definition is "a complete record of data stored on an infrequently used medium." Drawing a parallel to memory storage, both safeguard fleeting moments—fading over time yet waiting to be rediscovered. Through a visual narrative, this project captures the randomness and transient nature of memories, echoing the delicate, ephemeral essence of an analog photography archive.



Category / creative coding, interaction design

10

Verta Variable
Type Design
2025

Verta Variable

(click any item to visit the project site!)


is an experimental typeface that plays with variability to bring a little warmth back to the screen. These days, people send emails instead of handwritten letters, and somewhere along the way, things like birthday wishes or congratulations often lose their personal touch. Verta is a whimsical typeface, with exaggerated distortions and playful stretches that evoke emotion and foster a more lasting and engaging connection between sender and receiver.



Category / type design, interaction design
