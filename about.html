Yicing Huang   (she/her)


born in Taiwan and now based in Helsinki

currently studying Visual Communication Design at Aalto University


She is interested in the intersection of design, technology, and humanity, exploring storytelling through a gentle, sentimental approach. Inspired by the subtle moments in life and human connections, her works often emphasize introspective and interpersonal feelings.


As a multimedia designer working across print and digital media, she tries to bring design closer to real life within this hyperrealistic world.
